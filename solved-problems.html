Trip to Datça and Knidos

Solved by: Defne, Municipality Employee

A British couple wanting to travel from Marmaris to Datça couldn't find the bus schedules. They were stressed because they wanted to take this trip during their last days of vacation, and the information they found online wasn't up to date.

How We Solved It

Our local adviser Defne shared not only the correct bus schedules but also the best route to reach the ancient city of Knidos and the optimal visiting hours. The couple had lunch at a local restaurant recommended by Defne and discovered a secluded beach with amazing views. She also suggested an alternative route for the return journey - allowing the couple to see both Datça and Knidos in a single day.

Emergency Situations

Missed the Shuttle After a Boat Tour

Solved by: Hasan, Accountant

A German couple missed the last shuttle to their hotel after returning from a 12 Islands boat tour. It was late at night, and they didn't know how to get back. Moreover, they didn't speak Turkish and were having trouble communicating with taxi drivers.

How We Solved It

Hasan immediately stepped in, arranged a reliable taxi service, and ensured the couple reached their hotel at a reasonable price. He also communicated with the taxi driver, eliminating any language barrier. The couple sent their location to Hasan via WhatsApp, allowing him to direct the taxi driver to the right place. As a result, the couple was able to return safely to their hotel late at night.
